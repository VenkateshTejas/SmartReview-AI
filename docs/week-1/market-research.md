## Industry Analysis

### Key Statistics

**Review Impact on Business**
- 88% of consumers trust online reviews as much as personal recommendations
- 94% of consumers say negative reviews convinced them to avoid a business
- 72% of consumers won't take action until they read reviews
- 53% of consumers expect businesses to respond within a week
- 33% expect a response within 3 days

**Current Pain Points**
- Average business spends 50+ hours/month on review management
- 73% of actionable insights are missed in manual analysis
- 14-day average response time (best practice is 24-48 hours)
- 60% of businesses have no systematic review management process
- 82% rely on spreadsheets or manual tracking

**Financial Impact**
- One star increase in Yelp rating → 5-9% revenue increase
- Responding to reviews → 12% increase in booking inquiries
- Products with reviews → 270% higher conversion rate
- Negative reviews cost US businesses $75 billion annually
- 22% of customers lost after reading one negative review
